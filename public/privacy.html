Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

What we collect

When you upload a file, we store:

  • Your uploaded HTML file, encrypted with a key derived from your password
  • A one-way hash of your password (we never store the actual password)
  • A timestamp of when the page was created

What we don't collect

  • No accounts, no emails, no personal information
  • No cookies (other than what's strictly necessary)
  • No third-party trackers

Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-focused analytics tool. It collects no personal data, uses no cookies, and is fully GDPR compliant. We see aggregate page views, not individual users.

Encryption

Uploaded files are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM with a key derived from your password. Even we as the operators cannot read your content without your password. If you lose your password, your content is unrecoverable.

Data retention

Pages naturally expire after 30 days, unless otherwise specified. When a page expires, both the database record and the encrypted file are deleted from our servers.

Contact

Questions? Reach out at abuse@pagegate.io.
