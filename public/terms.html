Plans and limits

Anonymous pages are free, require no account, and are designed for one-off links. They expire after 1 day.

Free accounts include up to 3 active links, 7-day expirations, a dashboard, and password reset. Free pages cannot be manually deleted today; they expire on their own.

Pro includes up to 100 active links, custom URLs, custom expirations up to forever, edit-in-place, view counts, and no PageGate footer.

Billing and cancellation

Pro is billed through Stripe. You can cancel Pro through the billing portal.

If Pro lapses or is canceled, you keep Pro features through the end of the paid billing period. After that, PageGate provides a 30-day grace period where you can choose which 3 eligible pages to keep under the Free plan limits.

If you do not choose, PageGate may auto-pick the most recently viewed eligible pages. After grace ends, pages above the Free limit may be deleted. Kept pages may lose Pro-only settings such as custom URLs and longer expirations.
