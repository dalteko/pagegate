What PageGate is

PageGate is a tool for sharing HTML pages behind a password. The free anonymous tier requires no sign-up and creates pages that expire in 24 hours. Free accounts unlock a small dashboard for managing a few longer-lived pages. Pro accounts unlock custom URLs, longer expiration, public pages, and edit-in-place.

Expiration

Anonymous pages expire 24 hours after upload (or after 300 views, whichever comes first). Free account pages expire 7 days after upload. Pro pages have configurable expiration up to "never". We are not responsible for content lost due to expiration or service interruptions.

Account billing

Pro subscriptions are billed by Stripe at $5/month, recurring until cancelled. If your subscription lapses, you enter a 30-day grace period during which all Pro features keep working. At the end of grace, you must pick which 3 pages to keep (we'll auto-pick the most recently viewed if you ignore the prompt); the rest are permanently deleted.
